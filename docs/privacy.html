Privacy Policy

How jsonfold.dev handles visitor information.

Analytics

jsonfold.dev uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly web analytics service, to understand how visitors use the site and to improve the project.

GoatCounter does not use advertising cookies and is not used for behavioral tracking or advertising.

Information Collected

Analytics may include information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Referring websites
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Country or region (derived from IP address)
  • Date and time of visits

This information is used only to understand site usage and improve documentation, examples, and project resources.

Cookies

jsonfold.dev does not use advertising cookies. GoatCounter is configured in its default privacy-friendly mode.

Third-Party Services

The site may link to third-party services such as GitHub, PyPI, npm, Maven Central, Medium, and DEV Community. These services have their own privacy policies and practices.
